## Design Context

### Users
This product is for people managing their own personal finances across desktop and mobile. They use it to understand assets, spending, budgets, loans, and savings in one place so they can make clearer financial decisions with less friction.

### Brand Personality
Refined, trustworthy, and clear. The interface should feel credible first, premium second, and never noisy or gimmicky. The emotional goal is calm control rather than excitement for its own sake.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-first, editorial, and premium. Use layered blue-tinted neutrals, crisp slate contrast, and deliberate spacing to create a finance cockpit feel with stronger brand presence. Avoid neon, purple-heavy gradients, generic SaaS cards, and visual clutter.

### Design Principles
1. Turn financial complexity into calm, readable decisions.
2. Use premium visual hierarchy without sacrificing trust or clarity.
3. Keep forms and workflows low-friction, direct, and mobile-complete.
4. Let decoration support the product story instead of competing with data.
5. Favor polished light surfaces, subtle depth, and intentional rhythm over flashy effects.
